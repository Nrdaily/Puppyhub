FAQ

How do I adopt a puppy from PuppyHub's Website?

To adopt a puppy from our website, please browse through our available puppies and contact us directly for more information. We will guide you through the adoption process, including filling out an adoption application, meeting the puppy, and finalizing the adoption agreement.

What is the cost of adopting a puppy?

The cost of adopting a puppy varies depending on the breed, size, and other factors. Please contact us directly if any doubts for the prices on our website for specific pricing information.

Are your puppies vaccinated and health-checked?

Yes, all our puppies receive thorough health checks and vaccinations before they are adopted. We prioritize the health and well-being of our puppies and ensure they are up to date on their vaccinations.

Do you offer any guarantees or warranties with your puppies?

Yes, we offer a limited health guarantee for our puppies. Please contact us for more details on our guarantee policy.

Can I visit the puppies before making a decision

Yes, we encourage potential adopters to visit the puppies in person to ensure a good match. However, please note that visits may be subject to availability and scheduling.

Do you offer shipping or delivery services for puppies?

We prefer adopters to pick up their puppies in person to ensure their safety and well-being during transportation. However, we may be able to arrange shipping or delivery on a case-by-case basis. Additional fees may apply.

Are your breeding dogs health-tested?

Yes, our breeding dogs undergo thorough health screenings and genetic testing to ensure they are healthy and free from hereditary conditions.

Do you provide training for puppies?

Yes, we offer puppy training services to help your new furry family member develop basic obedience skills and proper behavior. Please contact us for more information on our training services and pricing.

Can you assist with rehoming a dog?

Yes, we provide rehoming assistance to help find suitable homes for dogs in need. If you need to rehome your dog or have found a dog in need of a new home, please reach out to us for guidance and support.
